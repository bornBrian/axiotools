# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 9, 2026

## Overview
AxioTools ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## What Information We Collect

1. **Files You Upload**
   - We do NOT store your files after processing
   - Files are processed temporarily and then **automatically deleted**
   - All processing happens on our servers, files are never shared with third parties

2. **Browser Data** (via Google Analytics)
   - Page views, device type, location (country level)
   - This helps us understand which tools are most popular
   - Google handles this data according to their privacy policy

3. **Cookies**
   - We use settings cookies stored in your browser locally
   - Google AdSense may use cookies for targeted ads

## Your Settings
- Settings are saved in your browser's local storage
- We don't send your settings to any server
- You can clear them anytime

## Security
- All connections use HTTPS (encrypted)
- Files are processed securely
- We don't require personal information or login

## Third Parties
- **Google Analytics**: Tracks site usage (anonymized)
- **Google AdSense**: Serves ads (may use cookies)
- **Vercel**: Hosts our website
- **Railway**: Hosts our backend

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Know what data we collect
- Request we delete our analytics data
- Opt-out of ads (browser ad blockers)
